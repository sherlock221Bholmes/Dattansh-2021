About The Event

Data odyssey: Voyage through data

A huge amount of data gets generated at an alarming rate, daily, on every reach of what we do. With the understanding and proper utilization, this data offers an opportunity to make decisions solely based on information, not the emotions, and to analyze the previous happenings or situations for an accurate outcome.

The ability to channelize this data and use it for the advancement of our lives is at our fingertips. While questing for the best practices and results, data goes through multiple processes like extraction, preparation, analysis and interpretation.

‘Data Odyssey’ is about exploring this venture through the experience.

Dattansh provides a platform to have an expert outlook into the fascinating world of data analytics by participating in various competitions and ultimately know about the journey of data in real life and industrial use cases by interacting with adept speakers.

We invite you to come and be a part of this initiative to lead future professionals in the direction of change and evolution driven by data.
